Welcome to ML Strength, Australia's newest brand in health and fitness!

ML Strength is a Brisbane-based health and fitness company with clubs located in Ashgrove, Brisbane City, Chermside, Graceville, and Westlake. We believe in making fitness a way of life and offer cutting-edge exercise options, including group fitness classes and one-on-one personal training sessions for weight loss, circuit training, boxing, and karate.

Our trainers are all Les Mills accredited instructors and have Bachelor Degrees in Human Movements.

We are a family operated business that prides itself on providing a refreshing change from the traditional health club environment. Our gyms have an old school feel with a new school attitude and are open 24/7, staffed all the time.

Our Owner and Philosophy

ML Strength's owner, Maurice Lidman, has over three decades of experience in boxing as an amateur competitor and coach. His passion for the sport is evident in the fun, exciting, and highly energized atmosphere of our boxing classes.

Whether you're a beginner or an intermediate level boxer, Maurice's coaching will help you achieve your fitness goals while providing one of the best cardiovascular workouts available.
