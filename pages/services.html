OUR SERVICES

At ML Strength, we believe that fitness is not just a fad, but a way of life.
Our experienced trainers are all Les Mills accredited and have Bachelor Degrees in Human Movements, ensuring that you receive the highest level of training and guidance.

We offer a wide range of group fitness classes, including popular options like Zumba, Body Pump, Yoga, and Pilates, as well as more specialized classes like boxing and karate.
Our class timetable changes constantly, so be sure to check our website for the latest schedule at each of our clubs. In addition to group fitness classes, we also offer one-on-one personal training sessions for weight loss, circuit training, boxing, and karate. Our trainers will work with you to develop a customized workout plan that is tailored to your individual needs and goals.

At ML Strength, we pride ourselves on our 'no obligation, no pressure' attitude. Unlike other health clubs, we do not employ annoying telemarketers or membership consultants. We believe that our gyms speak for themselves, and our members are our best advertisement. We welcome you to visit any of our gyms at your own pace, with no pressure whatsoever. And with our $10 casual visit rate, you can try us out without any long-term commitment.
